Legate My Car – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 8 November 2025

Legate My Car (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information within the Legate My Car mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information that is necessary to deliver the core functionality of the App:

  • Account and authentication data: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google profile identifier.
  • Lost car reports: Information you submit about a vehicle, including chassis number, plate number, car name/model, color, last known location, and contact phone number.
  • Device identifiers and logs: Basic technical information automatically collected by our analytics, such as device model, operating system version, error logs, and usage statistics.
  • Location data: The App does not request continuous GPS tracking. A location description is captured only when you provide it as part of a lost car request.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts.
  • Create, update, and display lost car listings.
  • Facilitate communication between car owners, volunteers, and community managers.
  • Monitor App performance and resolve technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

Where the EU or UK privacy laws apply, we rely on these legal bases:

  • Consent for optional analytics (if enabled).
  • Performance of a contract to provide the App services.
  • Legitimate interests to ensure network and information security.
  • Legal obligation as required by applicable law.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted vendors that assist with hosting, analytics, or customer support, under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: When required to comply with laws, regulations, or valid legal processes, or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, sale, or transfer of assets, user information may be part of the transferred assets, with prior notice and continued protection.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. You can request deletion of your account or individual lost car submissions by contacting us at the email below.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information. You may also have the right to object to processing or data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email provided in the “Contact Us” section.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected personal data from a child under 13 (or under the minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction), we will promptly delete it. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information may contact us to request deletion.

8. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses, are in place to protect your personal information when it is transferred internationally.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page with a new effective date. If changes are significant, we may notify you through the App or other appropriate channels. Continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.
